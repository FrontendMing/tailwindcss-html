What is a Solar Generator?

A solar generator uses solar panels to capture renewable energy from the sun and store it as electricity in a portable power station. It emits no toxic gases, operating extremely cleanly - causing no harm to the environment. Solar generators provide a reliable green energy solution whether you’re on the go, off-the-grid, or need emergency power during a blackout.
